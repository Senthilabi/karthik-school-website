# Karthik Matriculation Hr. Sec. School Website

Official website for Karthik Matriculation Higher Secondary School, Chennai.

## About

Karthik Matriculation Higher Secondary School has been providing quality education since 1997. Our website showcases our facilities, academic programs, and provides information for prospective students and parents.

## Features

- **Responsive Design**: Works seamlessly on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices
- **Modern UI**: Clean and professional design with smooth animations
- **Complete Information**: Details about academics, facilities, admissions, and contact information
- **Gallery**: Visual showcase of school life and activities
- **Online Admission Form**: Easy application process for new students

## Pages

- **Home**: Welcome page with key highlights
- **About**: School history, vision, mission, and leadership
- **Academics**: Curriculum details and higher secondary groups
- **Facilities**: Information about school infrastructure
- **Gallery**: Photos of school activities and events
- **Admissions**: Admission process, requirements, and online application
- **Contact**: Contact information and location details

## Technology

- Pure HTML, CSS, and JavaScript
- No external dependencies
- Fast loading and SEO optimized

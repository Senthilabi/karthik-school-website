About Us

Since its inception in 1997, Karthik Matriculation Higher Secondary School has embarked on a transformative journey, beginning with the introduction of kindergarten and primary classes. Through unwavering dedication and continuous efforts, we have evolved into a distinguished Matriculation Higher Secondary School.

Our commitment lies in fostering an enriching environment that promotes holistic development, where students are empowered to explore their potential and strive for excellence in every facet of their lives.

Academic Programs

üéì Curriculum Framework

Kindergarten - Kreedo Curriculum: Our kindergarten program focuses on Literacy skills (Language), Numeracy skills (Maths), Themes (EVS), Sensorial skills and Cognitive skills. All these areas of development begin with play or activities first through our Kreedo lab, helping improve the socio-emotional quotient in children.

Primary to Higher Secondary - Samacheer Kalvi: We follow the Samacheer Kalvi syllabus, ensuring comprehensive education aligned with state board standards while incorporating modern teaching methodologies.
